Admission Process

Key steps in the MBBS abroad admission process:

1.Research and choose a university:

Select a reputable medical university in a preferred country, checking its NMC approval status.

2.Check eligibility criteria:

Meet the academic requirements like minimum percentage in PCB subjects in class 12th.

3.NEET Qualified:

Indian students must qualify the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) to apply for MBBS abroad.

4.Application process:

Fill out the online application form on the university website. Upload scanned copies of required documents like mark sheets, NEET scorecard, passport. Pay the application fee

5.Entrance exams (if required):

Some universities may conduct additional entrance exams or interviews.

6.Admission offer:

Upon successful review of your application, you will receive an admission letter from the university.

7.Student visa application:

Apply for a student visa to the chosen country with required documents including the admission letter, passport, and financial proof.

8.Tuition fee payment:

Once the visa is approved, pay the first installment of the tuition fee to secure your admission
